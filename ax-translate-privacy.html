Privacy Policy – AX translate

Last updated: 6 May 2025

1. Information We Collect

AX translate does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All text entered for translation is processed locally on the user’s device and is never uploaded to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

The application does not incorporate third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, ad networks, or cloud services that collect data.

3. Permissions

AX translate is distributed as a packaged Win32 (“Desktop Bridge”) app and therefore requests the runFullTrust capability. This capability is required solely to execute native desktop code and does not, by itself, transmit data outside the device.

4. Children’s Privacy

AX translate is not directed to children under 13 and intentionally collects no data from them.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change what data we collect or how we handle it, we will update this document and bump the “Last updated” date above.

6. Contact

Questions? Please write to info@axbrains.com.
